FayMay เฟย์เมษ์

Fay (เฟย์ กัญญาพัชร) and May (เมษ์ ญด) are Thai GL actresses under Idol Factory. Their on-screen couple name is FayMay (เฟย์เมษ์) and their first acting project as lead actresses is My Marvelous Dream is You (ฝันรักห้วงนิทรา), also known as DreamGL or Dream The Series.
